About the work

ESRI Thailand specializes in providing spatial data and maps for Thailand and Southeast Asia, including both base maps and navigation maps. During my time at ESRI Thailand, I had the privilege of working with the navigation map team to ensure that the data we provided to our customers was of the highest quality. It was essential that the navigation map data was accurate and up-to-date, as users rely on it to navigate to their destinations safely and efficiently.
I collaborated closely with both the development and operations teams to drive this mission and turn it into a reality.

Impacts

- Boosted company revenue by improving product efficiency by 30% through early bug detection and providing scalable solutions.
- By designing a data visualization dashboard, I successfully streamlined the production process, allowing for improved communication between the input, development, and production teams. This dashboard informs the teams of the status of the input data and ensures transparency in the process, eliminating the need for back-and-forth communication to confirm any changes made by the input team, which ultimately results in faster production.

Learnings

- How to collaborate with internal teams (developers and product managers) to identify system requirement to improve the testing process.
